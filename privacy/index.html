The Service involves the storage of Data about a company or individual. That Data can include personal information as well as company data.

"Personal information" is information about an identifiable individual, and may include information such as the individual's name, email address and telephone numbers as well as business data.

ReddReports collects personal information directly from You when You:

  • register to use the Service,
  • use the Service,
  • post to the ReddReports Community forum or on our blog,
  • contact the ReddReports support team, and
  • visit our Website.

You can always choose not to provide Your personal information to ReddReports, but it may mean that we are unable to provide You with the Service.

Through Your use of the Service, ReddReports may also collect information from You about someone else. If You provide ReddReports with personal information about someone else, You must ensure that You are authorized to disclose that information to ReddReports and that, without ReddReports taking any further steps required by applicable data protection or privacy laws, ReddReports may collect, use and disclose such information for the purposes described in this Policy.

ReddReports uses cookies

In providing the Service, ReddReports utilizes "cookies". A cookie is a small text file that is stored on Your computer for record-keeping purposes. A cookie does not identify You personally or contain any other information about You but it does identify Your computer.

We and some of our affiliates and third-party service providers may use a combination of "persistent cookies" (cookies that remain on Your hard drive for an extended period of time) and "session ID cookies" (cookies that expire when You close Your browser) on the Website to, for example, track overall site usage, and track and report on Your use and interaction with ad impressions and ad services.

You can set your browser to notify You when You receive a cookie so that You will have an opportunity to either accept or reject it in each instance. However, You should note that refusing cookies may have a negative impact on the functionality and usability of the Website.
