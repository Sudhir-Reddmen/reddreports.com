Features

Connect and apply business rules to any data source

The platform can connect to cloud databases, web applications, import on-premises databases and files, and integrate with cloud storage. We apply your business rules to the data and present the results via data cubes, reports and dashboards.

Without ReddReports, companies must either build custom data movement programs or export data manually to integrate these data sources. It is expensive and hard to integrate and maintain such systems. These systems also often lack the enterprise grade monitoring, alerting, and controls that a fully managed service can offer.

Create, Interact and Customize

Insights on the go. Have the power to explore your data via interactive dashboards and reports from your browser. Drill down, highlight, and filter your data on the fly. Customize views with intuitive web authoring of new reports and dashboards.

Share Information

Data, reports or dashboards can be securely shared to colleagues or customers. Publish data, reports and dashboards with periodic data refreshes to drive timely decision making with up to date information.

"Spreadsheet-like" Interface to view, add/edit detailed transaction and master data

New users will feel comfortable working with ReddReports as the interface closely resembles a spreadsheet. You can use the "spreadsheet-like" interface for easy data collection (add/edit), analysis and reporting.

You can also use this interface to maintain master data information such as custom Product hierarchy, GL hierarchy, Sales Territories, Geography and so on.

Interactive data analysis using a drag and drop pivot table interface

ReddReports provides an intuitive interface to drag and drop various data elements for on-the-fly data analysis, filter and sort data, and finally save a view of the report for future reference or share with other users.

Printer-friendly Export

You can export pivot tables to CSV, XLS, XLSX, PDF, HTML and image formats. Reports and dashboards can be exported to PDF or HTML. PDF export generates a printer friendly document.

Single Source of Truth

Centralize access to your data in the cloud. Publish dashboards, reports, data cubes and make it easy for everyone to work from a single source of truth.

Scheduled Deliveries

Let the data come to you with scheduled dashboards and reports. ReddReports supports report delivery via E-mail, FTP and sFTP. The platform also supports implementation of custom delivery modules tailored to customer requirements.

Understand Change Over Time

One-off reports are valuable but ongoing reports are truly powerful, as they allow you to make changes and monitor the impact. Access historical data to view growth trends and opportunities over time.

Compare and Contrast

Comparing different events enables you to unlock a greater understanding of your target audience, and what appeals to them.

Always up-to-date

Managing updates and deploying patches is a thing of the past. Focus on your business reporting with the latest features rolled out to you with minimal downtime.

Security and Backups

Data is securely stored and accessed. Only relevant people have access to the information via secured logins and row level security. Data backups are automated behind the scenes with no additional setup required.
